# WM-0161 Work Report

## Outcome

Established a formal capability-gated playable-product recovery roadmap for
Wuming Town. Historical M0-M8 closeouts remain valid as scoped technical and
content evidence, but they no longer represent current product completion.
Product progress now runs through PR-0 to PR-7 and requires one integrated
authoritative `GameSession`, autonomous perceptible residents, real player
input and commands, authoritative save/load, a reviewer-observed 30-minute
vertical slice, and only then product/platform gate re-entry.

WM-0154 is formally blocked by its `qa-performance` owner because its legacy
visual-evidence scope cannot repair the confirmed real-input, continuous-time,
perceptible-action and integrated-world product failures. WM-0155 and WM-0156
remain proposed behind that blocker. WM-0162 is the only new downstream task;
it remains proposed and performs PR-1 architecture/inventory/task-DAG planning
only after WM-0161 is verified and done.

## Decisions And Deviations

- Created one next planning task instead of a full PR-1 through PR-7 task DAG.
  This deliberately counters the prior task-count local optimum and keeps each
  phase dependent on observed product evidence.
- Did not supply calendar dates. WM-0162 must first inventory integration debt
  and produce capacity assumptions; the roadmap records relative size and
  state names without pretending the critical path is already estimated.
- Used the task owner role to block WM-0154 rather than manually mutating its
  state. The blocker was routed to and acknowledged by project-director.
- Generic `git-flow` project configuration was not initialized because the
  repository already has a stricter Wuming Town task/control workflow and this
  documentation task did not authorize adding a duplicate workflow layer.

## Remaining Risks / Follow-Up Tasks

- The largest unknown is how much M1-M8 scenario-specific ownership can be
  reused inside one long-lived `GameSession` without broad rewrites. WM-0162
  owns that inventory and architecture decision.
- WM-0154 must remain blocked until WM-0162 explicitly reconciles or replaces
  its evidence scope. It cannot be unblocked merely because this roadmap is
  merged.
- No product improvement occurs until PR-1 implementation tasks are separately
  planned, reviewed, promoted and executed.
- Final artwork, audio, broader content and public platform decisions remain
  later gates; they must not displace integrated playability work.
