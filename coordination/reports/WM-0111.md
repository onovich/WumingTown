## UI/I18n Coverage Audit

The reviewed future M8 prompt covered localization only at a broad M8
completion level and did not define the Owner's six required gates. WM-0111
therefore adds Product UI, Responsive Layout, Localization, Visual Identity,
First Playability and Accessibility gates as mandatory M8 closeout conditions.

Screenshot evidence from the Owner shows:

- default screen reads as an M6 diagnostics/product-gate harness;
- player-facing text is mostly English;
- diagnostics and product-gate copy are visible by default;
- windowed/fullscreen layouts have dense panel stacking and awkward scroll
  regions;
- first-time players cannot clearly infer phase, action affordances, next goal
  or what is gameplay versus diagnostics.
