## Outcome

Blocked after the single systems-architect-authorized quiet-host resume. Two
consecutive six-second admission windows passed at 24.41 and 22.53 percent host
busy, with maximum single-process shares of 6.18 and 6.14 percent. `ACShadows`
was absent, no benchmark Node process was active and the worktree was clean.

Exactly one unchanged-harness `corepack pnpm bench` full-suite command then ran
at `fe4e8f` and exited one. `entity-store` is the only failure at 355.852 ms,
45.54 percent above the unchanged 244.507 ms baseline. `map-dirty` is a warning
at 1.109 ms / +10.89 percent; M4 is OK at 4.677 ms / +1.43 percent; M3, M5 and
`spatial-index` are also OK. Every invariant matches. Because the full command
was non-zero, no filtered benchmark, retry, completion-only check or
`taskctl complete` followed.

This differs materially from the earlier high-load full run, where
`entity-store`, M4 and `map-dirty` all failed. The quiet window removed the M4
and `map-dirty` blocking failures, but `entity-store` remained a cross-load
failure. Harness commit `7434d6f` still runs each suite in its own fail-closed
Node child and preserves workloads, fixtures, invariants, baseline medians,
10/20 percent thresholds, 9 samples and 2 warmups. No waiver was used and no
product file changed.
