# WM-0183 Work Report

## Outcome

WM-0183 delivers claim-ready, version-bound simulation owners and reusable JobCore custody for the four historical autonomous drivers. Food, Rest, Medical, Hauling, and Lamp facts now expose exact caller-owned reads; ItemStack, Storage, Need, Health, and Medical expose reviewed prepare/commit seams; JobCore owns generation-aware reusable tokens, owner indexing, rollback origin state, and strict snapshot/hash/restore closure.
