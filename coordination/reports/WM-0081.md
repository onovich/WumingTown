  while rebuilt surfaces are diagnostic read-only hashes.
- The M5 command tail is reconstructed inside the focused harness from the
  approved WM-0080 scenario reasons rather than importing WM-0080 private
  helpers. Continuity is still tied to the canonical scenario
  `commandStreamHash`.
- Review repair: the task packet now explicitly authorizes
  `tools/sim-replay-diagnostics-lib.mjs`, `tools/sim-replay-test.mjs` and
  `coordination/reports/WM-0081-plan.md`. The first two files are necessary to

  file is required by the repository workflow before complex product edits.

  save/replay determinism and migration boundaries are not suitable for Spark.
