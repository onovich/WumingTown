# Mechanics research provenance and IP boundary

## Primary research used to understand the reference genre

- RimWorld Wiki for player-visible mechanics: https://rimworldwiki.com/
- Public decompilation mirror used only to inspect abstract architecture and call boundaries: https://github.com/Chillu1/RimWorldDecompiled
- Ludeon official update notes for current performance changes: https://ludeon.com/blog/
- Dubs Performance Analyzer source for community-observed hotspot categories: https://github.com/Dubwise56/Dubs-Performance-Analyzer

## Mandatory clean-room rule

The executing team may use the above only to understand general ideas such as fixed ticks, definition databases, jobs, reservations, dirty spatial caches and storyteller pacing. It must not copy code, identifiers, text, assets, exact formulas, event catalogs or proprietary data. Implement from this repository's independent specs and naming. See `docs/06_engineering/09_clean_room_ip_policy.md`.

## Cultural research

`无明镇` is a fictional world informed by broad East Asian vernacular-life and strange-tale traditions, not a direct simulation of a real dynasty, religion or ethnic community. Content authors must record sources, separate attested practice from invention, and seek specialist review before marketing specific cultural claims. See `docs/03_world/07_cultural_research_policy.md`.
